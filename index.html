i built pravaas.org where high quality mentor-ship and advice is made available to every student anywhere in this world for free

while browsing through twitter i discovered that i could do a better job (using interesting spam filtering algorithms) of connecting people with cancer and people who know people with cancer on twitter than twitter itself so i built tweetandbeat.com

while playing with the twitter API, i discovered a nifty way of marketing websites and services on twitter and so i built linkybinky.com. i haven't released this to the public yet.

i figured there has to be a more terse way of uploading images on twitter and so i built twimgr.com where you can embed your status update inside the URL of the image that you upload. yes it is very geeky.

i built ps3 is better than cake to help ps3 users on twitter meet others just like them. i also felt that my UI was a lot more intuitive than the one provided by twitter.
